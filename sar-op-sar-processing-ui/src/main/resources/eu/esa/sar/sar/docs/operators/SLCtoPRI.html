Convert Complex to Detected Ground Range

This tools converts a Single Look Complex product in slant range into a detected product in ground range.
This is simply a general graph which calibrates, multilooks and applies sland range to ground range conversion.
The processing may not be identical to that produced by the ground segments for generating PRI or GRD products.


For Sentinel-1, please use the S1 SLC to GRD graph.

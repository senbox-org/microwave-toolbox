Import RADARSAT Constellation Mission Product

Radasat Constellation Mission (RCM) is a SAR constellation of 3 satellites by the CSA. Data is acquired based on the observation scenarios of the government of Canada and cannot be tasked by the public. Archive data will be available free and open.

Operational: 2019-06-12 to present

Frequency Band: C-band

Acquisition Modes: Stripmap, ScanSAR, Spotlight, Ship Detection

Product Types:  

  • SLC Single Look Complex
  • GRC Ground Range Complex
  • GCC Ground Range Geocoded
  • GRD Ground Range Detected
  • GCD Ground Range Geocoded Detected

Polarizations: Single Pol (HH), Dual Pol and Full Pol (HH+HV+VV+VH), Compact Pol (RH+RV)

Opening products: Products can be opened by selecting the manifest.safe file. Opening zipped products (*.zip) directly is also supported.

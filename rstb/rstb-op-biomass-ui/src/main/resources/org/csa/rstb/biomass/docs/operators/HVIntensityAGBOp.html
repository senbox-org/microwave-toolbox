HV-Intensity AGB Fit Operator

Empirical Above-Ground Biomass retrieval from a single HV cross-polarisation backscatter band. Three model families are supported:

ModelFormulaReference
Mitchard 2009 exponentialAGB = a * (1 - exp(-b * σ0))Mitchard et al. 2009
Saatchi 2011 power lawAGB = a * σ0bSaatchi et al. 2011
Decibel exponentialAGB = exp(a + b * σ0dB)generic regional fit

Inputs

  • One HV-pol backscatter band (linear or dB; see inputIsDb).
  • Optional forest mask: pixels not in forest are written as no-data.

Output

  • AGB band, Mg/ha. Pixels with HV below the floor or AGB above the saturation clip are masked to no-data (-1).

Recommended starting coefficients

  • Mitchard 2009 Cameroon savanna L-band HV: a = 31.6, b = 0.069.
  • Pantropical L-band HV log-log (Saatchi 2011): a = 30, b = 0.5.

Coefficients are region-specific; recalibrate against local field plots (via the Chave Allometric operator) before operational use.

Reference

Mitchard E. T. A. et al. (2009). Measuring biomass changes due to woody encroachment and deforestation / degradation in a forest-savanna boundary region of central Africa using multi-temporal L-band radar backscatter. Remote Sensing of Environment 113(7), 1453-1461. DOI.

1. Dual-pol Scattering Powers Formulation (Factorization method)

The dual-pol scattering powers (factorization method) are calculated from the processed dual-pol 2x2 wave covariance C2 matrix.

This method is based on the two dual-pol indices: DpRBI and DpRSI. Once the two indices are computed, they are employed in the Scattering Power Factorization Framework (SPFF) proposed in [1] to obtain three dual-pol scattering power components: "Dihedral-like" (Pd-l), "Surface-like" (Ps-l), and a residue (Pu) [2].

It is important to note that hills or mountains facing the radar produce strong backscatter and are often confused with built-up areas, resulting in high DpRBI values. Therefore, we employ a slope layer to mask such areas to avoid misinterpretations over such areas. Please include a slope layer along with the C2 matrix (Raster -> DEM Tools -> Compute Slope and Aspect).

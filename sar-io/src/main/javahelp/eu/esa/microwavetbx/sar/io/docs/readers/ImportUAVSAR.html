Import UAVSAR Product

Operator: NASA/JPL UAVSAR — airborne L-band SAR.

Operational: 2007–present.

Platform: Gulfstream-III (G-III) aircraft.

Frequency Band: L-band (1.2575 GHz centre, 80 MHz bandwidth).

Acquisition Modes: Quad-pol Stripmap; PolInSAR / Repeat-pass Interferometry; tomographic stacks.

Product Types:

  • SLC (Single Look Complex, native slant range)
  • MLC (Multi-Look Complex, 3×12 looks)
  • GRD (Ground Range Detected)
  • HGT (Synthetic ground-projected DEM heights)
  • ANN (Annotation file describing scene geometry — required by the reader)

Polarizations: Full quad-pol (HH, HV, VH, VV).

Notes: UAVSAR is delivered as flat binary cubes accompanied by a plain-text ANN annotation file describing dimensions, geometry, look angles, and pixel spacing. The reader expects the ANN file alongside the data files. Geocoded UAVSAR products are pre-projected to a regular lat/lon grid (no per-pixel geocoding step required); SLC products are in the native slant-range geometry and require Range-Doppler Terrain Correction for map-projected outputs.

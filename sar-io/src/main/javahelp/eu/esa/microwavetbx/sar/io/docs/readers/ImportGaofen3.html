Import Gaofen-3 Product

Gaofen-3 is a Chinese SAR satellite constellation launched in 2016. It has a C-band SAR sensor with single, dual and quad polarizations.

Operational: 2016-08-10 to present

Frequency Band: C-band

Acquisition Modes:

  • Spotlight [SL]
  • Ultra-fine stripmap [UFS]
  • Fine stripmap [FSI]
  • Wide fine stripmap [FSII]
  • Standard stripmap [SS]
  • Narrow ScanSAR [NSC]
  • Wide ScanSAR [WSC]
  • Global observation [GLO]
  • Quad-pol stripmap [QPSI]
  • Wide quad-pol stripmap [QPSII]
  • Wave [WAV]
  • Extended incidence angle [EXT]

Product Types:  

  • Single-look complex (SLC)
  • Single-look image products (SLP)
  • Multi-look image products (MLP)
  • Geocoded products (SGC)

Polarizations: Single, Dual and Quad polarizations.

Opening products: Products can be opened by selecting the metadata file. Opening zipped products (*.zip) directly is also supported.

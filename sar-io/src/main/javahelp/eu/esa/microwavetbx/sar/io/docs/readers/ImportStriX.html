Import StriX

Import StriX Product

Operator: Synspective Inc. (Japan) — StriX commercial SAR constellation.

Operational: StriX-α launched December 2020; operational fleet from 2022.

Frequency Band: X-band.

Acquisition Modes: Stripmap, Sliding Spotlight (sub-metre).

Product Types:

  • SLC (Single Look Complex, range-Doppler grid)
  • GRD (Ground Range Detected, multilooked)
  • GEC (Geocoded Ellipsoid Corrected)
  • GTC (Geocoded Terrain Corrected)

Polarizations: Single-pol HH or VV.

Notes: The reader parses the StriX product manifest (XML metadata + GeoTIFF imagery) and applies the calibration LUTs published in the auxiliary files. Use Apply Orbit File with caution — precise orbits are not always delivered with commercial X-band products; the bundled state vectors are generally sufficient for InSAR baselines below a few weeks.

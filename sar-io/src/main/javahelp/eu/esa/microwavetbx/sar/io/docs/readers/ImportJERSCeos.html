Japanese Earth Resources Satellite 1 (JERS-1) was a satellite launched in 1992 by the Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA). It carried a L-band SAR and a nadir point optical and side looking optical sensors.

Operational: 1992-02-11 to 1998-10-01

Frequency Band: L-band

Acquisition Modes: Stripmap

Product Types:  

  • GEC Geocoded Ellipsoid Corrected
  • PRI Precision Image
  • SLC Single Look Complex

Polarizations: All modes are Single Pol (HH).

Opening products: Products can be opened by selecting the Volume file. Opening zipped products (*.zip) directly is also supported.

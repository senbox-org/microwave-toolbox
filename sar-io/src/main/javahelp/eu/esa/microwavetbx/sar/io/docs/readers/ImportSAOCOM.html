SAOCOM is an Argentine L-band SAR mission operated by CONAE, consisting of two satellites forming part of the Italian-Argentine SIASGE constellation (together with COSMO-SkyMed).

Satellites:

  • SAOCOM-1A (launched 2018-10-07, operational)
  • SAOCOM-1B (launched 2020-08-30, operational)

Frequency Band: L-band (1.275 GHz)

Acquisition Modes: Stripmap, TopSAR Narrow, TopSAR Wide, TopSAR Full, Spotlight

Product Types:

  • SLC (L1A) Single Look Complex - complex data in slant range
  • DI (L1B) Detected Image - multi-looked, slant range
  • GEC (L1C) Ground Ellipsoid Corrected - map projected
  • GTC (L1D) Ground Terrain Corrected - terrain corrected, map projected

File Format: XEMT metadata (.xemt XML) with GeoTIFF imagery (one .tif per polarization channel). Products may include calibration LUTs and noise annotations in the Calibration/ directory.

Polarizations: Single Pol, Dual Pol (HH+HV, VV+VH), Full Pol (HH+HV+VV+VH, Stripmap only), Compact Pol (RH+RV, LH+LV)

Opening products: Products can be opened by selecting the .xemt metadata file, the product directory, or a ZIP archive. The reader supports both Stripmap and TopSAR burst products.

Data Access:

  • CONAE Catalogue: https://catalogos.conae.gov.ar
  • ESA Third Party Mission programme
  • ASF DAAC: https://search.asf.alaska.edu

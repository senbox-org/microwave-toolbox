Import TerraSarX Product

TerraSAR-X is a German radar satellite operated by DLR and Airbus Defence and Space. This reader also supports TanDEM-X (including CoSSC bistatic products) and TanDEM-X individual products.

Operational: 2007-06-15 to present (TerraSAR-X), 2010-06-21 to present (TanDEM-X)

Frequency Band: X-band (9.65 GHz)

Acquisition Modes:  Stripmap, ScanSAR, Wide ScanSAR, Spotlight, High-Resolution Spotlight, Staring Spotlight

Product Types:

  • SSC Single Look Slant Range Complex (COSAR binary format)
  • MGD Multi Look Ground Range Detected (GeoTIFF format)
  • GEC Geocoded Ellipsoid Corrected (GeoTIFF format)
  • EEC Enhanced Ellipsoid Corrected with terrain correction (GeoTIFF format)
  • CoSSC Co-registered Single Look Slant Range Complex (TanDEM-X bistatic products)

Polarizations: Single Pol (HH or VV), Dual Pol (HH/VV, HH/HV, VV/VH)

Opening products: Products can be opened by selecting the main XML annotation file (e.g., TSX1_SAR__SSC*.xml or TDM1_SAR__CoSSC*.xml). Opening zipped products (*.zip) is also supported.
